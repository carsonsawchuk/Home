Welcome to
Nevada Electric Racing

Design, Manufacture, Test, Compete.

Nevada Electric Racing is a SAE Collegite Design Series (CDS) team that designs, manufactures, and builds an electric race car. Made up of students from all majors, we are divided into separate subgroups so our members can focus on their expertise while working with others to build leadership, project management, and communication skills. This competition allows us to apply knowledge outside the classroom.

Interested in joining?

Put theory into practice while learning valuable skills such as communication, project management, and leadership. No experience is necessary.
